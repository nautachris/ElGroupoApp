Location: MD 231 over Patuxent River | Photo Date: 1951
Description: Perched atop a crane on the west shore, this view overlooking the Patuxent River gives a sense of the bridge's 3300-foot length. Reinforced concrete beams supported on steel piles march across the river. The beams will carry the bridge's 56 spans. What will become the bridge's central movable swing span is barely visible as a darker grid that appears near the far end of the pile bents. In the foreground, barges provide platforms for construction.
